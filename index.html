Who I Am

Behind
the Lens

Content creator and storyteller based between Paris and Brussels. I cover destinations, wildlife and culture across formats — from short-form video to aerial photography. I self-organise press trips and work with partners who fit naturally into the projects I'm already building.

01

Projects first, partners second

I build the project, then bring in the right partners. Each collaboration has a reason to exist within the story — whether that's preparing a trip, documenting it, or reviewing the gear that made it possible.

02

Multi-format, cross-platform delivery

One trip, multiple angles. Drone aerials, short-form reels, on-the-ground storytelling, food, culture, wildlife — I cover a destination in full, across every format your audience lives on.

03

French-speaking reach, global vision

74% French-speaking audience across France, Belgium, Switzerland and Canada — engaged, travel-ready, and underserved by English-language creators covering international destinations.
